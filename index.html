About IEEE VR 2024

IEEE VR 2024, the 31st IEEE Conference on Virtual Reality and 3D User Interfaces will be held from March 16th through March 21th, 2024 at the Contemporary Resort, Walt Disney World, Orlando, Florida.

Our goal is to provide the best hybrid conference experience for physical and virtual attendees, learning from the historical footsteps of the successful physical or virtual experience of past IEEE VR conferences. Considering the possible time difference between on-site and online participants, we introduce a new double-time mode to the conference that records the live paper sessions and replays them after 12 hours (Shanghai night time) so that online participants may attend the conference at their convenient time anywhere on earth.
